PR Services for Hospitality and Tourism

We are a Public Relations firm that focuses on managing the reputation, image & communication of hospitality & tourism - related organizations such as hotels, restaurants, resorts and tourist destinations.

Home to a quirky creative bunch, Buzzy Bee ensures your business is our business. Our main goal is to create and maintain a positive image and reputation of the organization, attract and retain customers, and enhance their experience. This is achieved by using various communication strategies and tactics, such as media relations, event planning, crisis management, influencer marketing, social media, and content creation.

We work closely with your management and marketing team in executing effective communication to get your brand to where it needs to be. We work tirelessly to break the barrier of traditional media and digital to convey your story the best way we know how. Through monitoring and analyzing media coverage, customer feedback and industry trends to identify potential opportunities and challenges that may impact your business as a whole.

Hospitality PR is a vital component of the hospitality industry, as it helps organizations build and maintain a strong brand reputation, connect with customers, and drive revenue.

BRAND ACTIVATION

The goal of brand activation is to build brand awareness, create positive associations with the brand, and ultimately drive sales. It can take many forms, such as experiential marketing, sampling, influencer marketing, social media campaigns, events, and sponsorships. The objective is to create an immersive experience that connects consumers with the brand, generates interest, and encourages them to take action.

EVENT PLANNING

We organize and manage various types of events such as but not limited to conferences, product launches and campaigns. Event planning requires strong organizational and communication skills to generate media coverage and increase brand awareness. We cover all your worries from creativity, solving problems and adapting to changing circumstances.

INFLUENCER & COLLABORATION OUTREACH

By partnering with individuals who are trusted and respected by their followers, brands can tap into their audience and increase their reach and influence. Collaboration is also a good way to tap into a bigger audience base. Collaboration can lead to better outcomes for businesses. By working together, teams can achieve more than they could individually and create a positive, collaborative culture that fosters innovation, productivity, and engagement.

MEDIA RELATIONS

Media Relations are the hospitality industry's best friend! It covers everything from brand awareness, building credibility and a positive relationship within the industry and media. We do this through a number of strategies, including engaging in regular outreach, developing key messages, press releases and much more. Our goal is to ensure your brand achieves the public relations objective and generates positive coverage
